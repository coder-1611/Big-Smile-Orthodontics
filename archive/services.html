Our Services

Orthodontic Services in Round Rock

Comprehensive orthodontic care for patients of all ages, from traditional braces to the latest in clear aligner technology.

Traditional Braces

Most Popular

Traditional metal braces are the most common type of orthodontic treatment. They are made of high-grade stainless steel and use metal brackets attached to each tooth, connected by a thin archwire that puts gentle, constant pressure on the teeth to gradually move them into proper position.

Today's braces are smaller, more comfortable, and more efficient than ever before. They deliver reliable, precise results for all types of orthodontic cases.

Clear/Ceramic Braces

Discreet Option

Clear or ceramic braces work the same way as traditional metal braces, but use tooth-colored or clear brackets that blend with your natural tooth color. They are a popular choice for patients who want a more discreet orthodontic treatment option.

Ceramic braces offer excellent results while being less noticeable than metal braces, making them ideal for image-conscious teens and adults.

Schedule Consultation

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Clear Aligners

Invisalign uses a series of clear aligners worn to move teeth and is the most aesthetic option for those seeking orthodontic treatment without metal braces. It's easy to keep your teeth clean, much more comfortable, and there are no diet restrictions.

Patients wear aligners between 20 and 22 hours each day with progress check appointments approximately every eight weeks. The average treatment timeline with Invisalign is only 12-18 months, compared to traditional braces requiring 2-3 years.

Phase 1 Orthodontics

Early Intervention

Phase 1 treatment, also known as early orthodontic treatment, is designed for children between the ages of 6 and 10. This early intervention helps guide jaw growth, correct harmful oral habits, and make room for permanent teeth to come in properly. By addressing orthodontic issues early, we can often reduce the need for more extensive treatment later.

Retainers & Orthodontic Appliances

Post-Treatment

Retainers are essential for maintaining your beautiful new smile after orthodontic treatment. We provide custom-made retainers and various orthodontic appliances designed to keep your teeth in their corrected positions and ensure long-lasting results.
