FAQ

Common questions.

How long does treatment take? +

Most Invisalign cases finish in 12–18 months; metal and ceramic braces typically run 18–24 months. Your personalized plan will give you an exact timeline.

How much does orthodontic treatment cost? +

Every smile is different. After your free consultation we'll give you an exact price — factoring in your treatment, your insurance, and our $1,000 off new-patient offer. We also offer 0% APR in-house financing.

When should my child see an orthodontist? +

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends an initial evaluation by age 7. Early screening lets us catch growth issues we can gently guide — often reducing the need for bigger treatment later.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes — we offer flexible in-house 0% APR financing, and we also work with CareCredit for longer-term plans. Most of our patients pay in monthly installments.
