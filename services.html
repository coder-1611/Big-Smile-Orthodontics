Invisalign clear aligners

Virtually invisible · Removable · 12–18 months

Invisalign straightens your teeth with a series of clear, custom-made aligners — no brackets, no wires. Each set is worn for about one to two weeks, gently guiding your teeth into place.

Treatment begins with a digital 3D scan using our iTero scanner — no messy impressions. You'll see a preview of your future smile before starting. Progress visits are roughly every 8 weeks.

Traditional metal braces

The reliable classic · Colorful ties optional

Modern metal braces are smaller, lighter, and more comfortable than ever. They're the most effective option for complex cases — and a great choice for kids and teens who love mixing and matching colorful ties.

Each visit takes just 15–20 minutes every 6–8 weeks. Most patients complete treatment in 18–24 months.

Phase 1 early treatment

Ages 6–10 · Growth guidance

Phase 1 treatment is designed for children between 6 and 10. This early intervention helps guide jaw growth, correct harmful oral habits, and make room for permanent teeth to come in properly. By addressing issues early, we can often reduce the need for more extensive treatment later.

Retainers & appliances

Post-treatment · Custom-made

Retainers are essential for maintaining your beautiful new smile after orthodontic treatment. We provide custom-made retainers and various orthodontic appliances designed to keep your teeth in their corrected positions and ensure long-lasting results.

Life with braces

Care guide · Foods · Hygiene

Adjusting to life with braces is easier than you might think. Our team will provide you with all the guidance you need on caring for your braces, what foods to avoid, and how to maintain excellent oral hygiene throughout your treatment.

  • Brush & floss after every meal when wearing braces
  • Avoid hard & sticky foods — caramel, popcorn, ice chips, whole apples
  • Use orthodontic wax for any brackets that rub
  • Wear rubber bands exactly as directed for your best results
