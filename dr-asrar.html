About Dr. Asrar

Craftsmanship, unhurried.

Dr. Saba Asrar earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery and completed advanced training in orthodontics before opening Big Smile Orthodontics in Round Rock. She has since built a reputation for meticulous, digitally-driven treatment plans and a warm, unhurried chair-side manner.

She serves on the 3M Innovative Orthodontics Advisory Board — one of only a few orthodontists in the country selected for this role — helping shape the next generation of bracket and aligner technology. She is also a Platinum Invisalign Provider, recognized for the volume and excellence of cases she treats each year.

"A great smile changes how you feel about yourself. That's what gets me excited about coming to work every day."
