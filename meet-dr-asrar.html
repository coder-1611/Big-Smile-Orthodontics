About Our Doctor

Meet Dr. Saba Asrar, DDS

Top-rated orthodontist in Round Rock, TX — transforming smiles since 2007.

A Passion for Perfect Smiles

Round Rock's Trusted Orthodontist

Dr. Asrar is a top provider for orthodontic services in Round Rock and has been practicing since 2007. She has created thousands of beautiful smiles over the course of her career! Today, she is one of only a few orthodontists in the country chosen to be a part of the Advisory Board of the Council of Innovative Orthodontics for 3M (Solventum).

Dr. Asrar has a deep passion for orthodontics, an artistic eye for details, and a genuine care for her patients. Moreover, her unwavering commitment to providing the best experience and results further distinguishes her as a top-choice orthodontist.

Training & Professional Background

Education & Credentials

Advanced Education

Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency from University of Rochester School of Medicine & Dentistry, New York.

Orthodontic Specialization

Specialization in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics from the University of Nevada Las Vegas.

Academic Excellence

Graduated at top of dental school class. Omicron Kappa Upsilon award recipient (national dental honor society). Great character award from Howard University, Washington DC.
