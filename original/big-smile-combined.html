Orthodontic Services in Round Rock

Comprehensive orthodontic care for patients of all ages, from traditional braces to the latest in clear aligner technology.

Traditional Metal Braces

Most Popular · Proven Results

Traditional metal braces are the most common — and most versatile — form of orthodontic treatment. Made of high-grade stainless steel, they use small brackets bonded to each tooth and a thin archwire that applies gentle, continuous pressure to move teeth into their ideal position. Modern braces are smaller, smoother, and far more comfortable than the braces your parents wore.

Dr. Asrar uses the latest self-ligating and low-friction bracket systems for faster results, fewer appointments, and a more comfortable experience. Metal braces reliably correct everything from simple crowding to complex bite issues.

Benefits of Metal Braces

Effective for every case — from simple to complex
Most affordable treatment option
Customizable with colored elastics — a favorite for kids and teens
Always working — no need to remember to wear them
Average treatment: 18–24 months with adjustments every 6–8 weeks

Clear / Ceramic Braces

Discreet · 3M Clarity Advanced

Clear ceramic braces use tooth-colored brackets that blend naturally with your smile. At Big Smile Orthodontics, we use 3M Clarity Advanced Ceramic Brackets paired with a tooth-colored archwire — one of the most discreet fixed-braces systems available. They work just like traditional braces, but from a conversational distance most people won't even notice them.

Ceramic braces are ideal for image-conscious teens and adults who want reliable, fixed-appliance results without the look of metal. Many patients tell us they forget they're even wearing them.

Why Patients Choose Ceramic

Nearly invisible — brackets color-matched to your teeth
Smooth, rounded brackets for greater comfort
Slightly faster results than some clear-aligner cases
Fixed in place — no risk of losing or forgetting to wear
Adjustments every 6–8 weeks

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Virtually Invisible · Removable

Invisalign straightens your teeth with a series of clear, custom-made aligners — no brackets, no wires. Each set is worn for about one to two weeks before advancing to the next, gently guiding your teeth into place. Because they're virtually invisible and fully removable, Invisalign has become the go-to choice for adults and teens who want results without changing how they look, eat, or care for their smile.

Your treatment begins with a digital 3D scan of your teeth using our iTero scanner — no messy impressions. You'll even see a preview of your future smile before treatment starts. Progress visits are roughly every 8 weeks, and average treatment runs 12–18 months, significantly faster than traditional braces (2–3 years).

Invisalign Benefits

Virtually invisible — most people won't notice you're wearing them
Removable for eating, brushing, and flossing — no food restrictions
More comfortable — smooth plastic, no brackets or wires to irritate
Wear 20–22 hours per day, remove to eat and clean your teeth
Fewer office visits — check-ins about every 8 weeks
Free teeth whitening included with Invisalign treatment

We Also Offer

OrthoFX NiTime Aligners — the first FDA-approved night-only clear aligners, worn during sleep

Phase 1 Orthodontics

Early Intervention

Phase 1 treatment, also known as early orthodontic treatment, is designed for children between the ages of 6 and 10. This early intervention helps guide jaw growth, correct harmful oral habits, and make room for permanent teeth to come in properly. By addressing orthodontic issues early, we can often reduce the need for more extensive treatment later.

Post-Treatment

Retainers are essential for maintaining your beautiful new smile after orthodontic treatment. We provide custom-made retainers and various orthodontic appliances designed to keep your teeth in their corrected positions and ensure long-lasting results.
